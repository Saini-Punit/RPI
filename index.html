Events & Notes

Why Choose RPI?

The Rajasthan Paramedical Institute (RPI) offers a variety of professional paramedical and health courses to create a cadre of trained paramedical and applied health personnel for providing efficient health care at primary, secondary and tertiary levels. RPI provides a unique combination of opportunities, excellence, choices and experience. The Institute will provide quality and market oriented paramedical & health education to young aspirants. The institute will address the issue of scarcity of trained / skilled manpower in various sectors.
